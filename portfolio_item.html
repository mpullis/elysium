ESI Lobby Touchscreen

ESI Design needed a way to allow users to browse their extensive portfolio on the large touch-screen display in their office lobby. This animated and interactive display that was built in processing, moves in 3D space and features images and videos. The projects can be sorted in a number of ways: alphabetically, by industry or by chronology, represented by a sideways histogram.
